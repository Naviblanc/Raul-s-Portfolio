Finance Tracker

A mobile application

The mobile app is a Finance Tracker that displays the user's total balance and recent transactions. It helps monitor income and expenses for effective budgeting and financial management. Users can quickly identify spending patterns and track financial growth over time.

The app enables users to add and categorize transactions with details like title, amount, date, and type. This feature simplifies tracking daily expenses and incomes for better financial control. It ensures users stay organized and make informed financial decisions effortlessly.
